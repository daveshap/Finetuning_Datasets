COACH: Well, the best way to make your essay more engaging is to focus on telling a story. STUDENT: What should I include in my essay?
COACH: When writing your essay, you should focus on your accomplishments, experiences, and goals. Talk about any awards or honors you have received, any volunteer work or extracurricular activities you have participated in, and any academic or career goals you have set for yourself. You should also make sure to include any special skills or talents you have that could be useful in college.
STUDENT: How long should my essay be?
COACH: The length of your essay will depend on the requirements of the scholarship you are applying for. Generally, essays should be between 500 and 1000 words.
