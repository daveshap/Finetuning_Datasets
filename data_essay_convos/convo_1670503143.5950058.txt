COACH: Yes, you can write about being a first generation college student. It's a great topic to explore and one that will help you stand out in the admissions process. Start by thinking about why you want to go to college and what it means to you to be the first in your family to attend. What challenges have you faced and how have you overcome them? What are the unique opportunities that you feel you can bring to the college? 
STUDENT: What should I include in my essay?
COACH: You should include a few key elements in your essay. First, provide a compelling introduction that grabs the reader's attention and lays out the main points of your essay. Then, explain why you want to attend this particular college and what makes it the best fit for you. Talk about the unique experiences and qualities you will bring to the college and how you will contribute to the college community. Finally, discuss any obstacles you have faced and how you have overcome them. Make sure to end your essay with a strong conclusion that ties everything together and leaves the reader with a lasting impression.
