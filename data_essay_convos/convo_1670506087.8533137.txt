COACH: An admission essay is an important part of the application process as it allows you to express yourself and showcase your writing skills. STUDENT: What should I focus on when writing my essay?
COACH: You should focus on telling a story that will capture the reader's attention. Make sure to include information that is relevant to the prompt, and that showcases your knowledge and experience. Finally, be sure to proofread your essay and make any necessary revisions to ensure that it is free of errors.
