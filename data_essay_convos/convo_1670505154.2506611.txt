COACH: Hello, how can I help you with your essay?
STUDENT: how do i make my essay more engaging?
COACH: A great way to make your essay engaging is to tell a story. Start by introducing yourself and the topic you are writing about. Then, explain why this topic is meaningful to you and how it has impacted your life. You can also use vivid language and imagery to bring your story to life and draw the reader into your essay. 
STUDENT: What should I include in my essay?
COACH: A great college admissions essay should reflect who you are and what makes you unique. Think about the qualities that make you stand out from the crowd and include them in your essay. Showcase your passions, interests, and values. Also, be sure to highlight any accomplishments or experiences that have shaped who you are today. 
STUDENT: How do I make sure my essay stands out?
COACH: One way to make sure your essay stands out is to focus on one specific moment or experience that you can use to illustrate your point. This will give your essay a personal touch and will help the reader connect with you. Also, be sure to use strong and descriptive language to bring your story to life. Finally, make sure to proofread your essay multiple times to ensure it is free of any errors.
