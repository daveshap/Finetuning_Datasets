COACH: Hello, how can I help you with your essay?
STUDENT: what if i get rejected?
COACH: First of all, don’t worry about getting rejected. The college admissions process is a highly competitive one, and there are many factors that go into the decision. Your essay is just one part of the application, so focus on making it the best it can be.
STUDENT: What kind of research do I need to do?
COACH: Research is an important part of writing a college admissions essay. You should research the school you’re applying to, as well as any topics related to the essay prompt. You should also look into any relevant current events or trends that could help to inform your essay. Additionally, make sure to read up on the school’s mission and values to make sure your essay aligns with those.
