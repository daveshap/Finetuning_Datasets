STUDENT: How do I start my essay?
COACH: Starting an essay can be intimidating, but there are some strategies to make it easier. First, brainstorm and make a list of potential topics and ideas. Finally, start writing your essay, focusing on the main points of your outline and using vivid language to capture the reader's attention.
