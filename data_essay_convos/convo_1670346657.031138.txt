COACH: That's a great question! An admission essay is important because it's your chance to stand out from other applicants and show the admissions committee who you really are. Admissions officers look for essays that demonstrate your commitment to learning, your creativity, and your ability to think critically. STUDENT: Okay, that makes sense. But how do I make sure my essay stands out?
COACH: A great way to make your essay stand out is to focus on your unique experiences and perspectives. Think about what makes you different from other applicants, and use that to your advantage. Also, make sure you demonstrate your knowledge of the school and why you're a good fit for it. Finally, be sure to proofread your essay and make sure it's free of errors.
