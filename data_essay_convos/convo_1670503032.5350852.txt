STUDENT: What should my essay be about?
COACH: The essay is your chance to tell your story and explain why you are a good fit for the school. Think about what makes you unique and why you would be an asset to the college. Your essay should focus on your experiences, accomplishments, and goals. It should also demonstrate your interest in the school and highlight any special qualifications you have. Be sure to include specific examples that showcase your strengths and personality.
STUDENT: How long should my essay be?
COACH: Most college admissions essays should be between 500-1000 words. Make sure you use your word count wisely and focus on quality over quantity. Don't try to cram too much information into a short essay; instead, pick a few topics and go into detail. Also, be sure to proofread your essay and make sure it is free from errors.
