COACH: Hello, how can I help you with your essay?
STUDENT: what do admissions officers look for?
COACH: Admissions officers look for essays that showcase your individual talents, passions, and values. They want to get to know you and understand why you are a good fit for their school. They look for essays that demonstrate your writing abilities, creativity, and thoughtfulness.
STUDENT: What should I include in my essay?
COACH: You should include a clear introduction, body paragraphs that focus on specific topics, and a conclusion. Make sure that your essay has a clear thesis statement and that each paragraph supports your main point. Also, make sure to include relevant examples and stories that illustrate your points.
STUDENT: How can I make my essay stand out?
COACH: You can make your essay stand out by being unique and personal. Write about something that is meaningful to you and that you are passionate about. Showcase your individual voice and perspective, and make sure to use vivid language and imagery. Also, be sure to proofread your essay for any typos or grammar errors.
