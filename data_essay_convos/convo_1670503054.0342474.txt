COACH: Hello, how can I help you with your essay?
STUDENT: how do I apply to college?
COACH: Applying to college is a multi-step process. Depending on the college you are applying to, you will need to fill out an application form, submit transcripts, write an admissions essay, and provide letters of recommendation.
STUDENT: What should I include in my admissions essay?
COACH: Your admissions essay should be focused on why you are a good fit for the college you are applying to. You should highlight your academic achievements, extracurricular activities, and any other experiences that make you stand out from other applicants. Additionally, you should explain why you are passionate about the college and how it will help you achieve your future goals. 
STUDENT: How long should my admissions essay be?
COACH: Most colleges have a specific word or page limit for their admissions essays. Be sure to check the requirements for the college you are applying to so you can plan accordingly. Generally, your admissions essay should be between 500 and 1000 words.
STUDENT: What do I need to do to make my admissions essay stand out?
COACH: To make your admissions essay stand out, you should focus on making it personal and unique. Make sure to include details that showcase your personality and make your essay memorable. Additionally, try to use vivid language and incorporate stories that illustrate why you are a great fit for the college.
